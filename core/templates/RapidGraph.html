Instructions:

To create a new node, click on the Create new button.

To select any element, click on it without moving it. Click on it once more to deselect it, or click on the background to deselect everything.

To create an undirected edge, select two nodes and click the Connect selected button.

To delete a node (and any attached edges), drag it off of the graph surface.

To edit a label or weight of any element, double click on it.
